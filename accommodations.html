We will be taking over the beautiful Fountain Point Resort for the weekend of June 16 to June 19, 2022. This historic Northern Michigan resort includes 52 acres of waterfront property perfect for boating, swimming, bonfires, games and relaxation. Though the wedding is on Saturday, we encourage our guests to stay for the entire weekend, arriving as early as Thursday and departing as late as Sunday.

Resort Location

Fountain Point Resort 990 S Lake Leelanau Dr Lake Leelanau, MI 49653

Cottage and Main House Details

There are both cottages and rooms in the main house. The rooms in the main house are primarily two person rooms, bed and breakfast style. The cabins are a bit more communal, and sleep anywhere from four to 14 people. We will assign cottages and main house rooms once we have our confirmed guests, and will send you an email to make sure you are OK with your accommodations.

The cost to stay at Fountain Point will be a flat fee of $315 per person for the entire weekend. Please note that the cost is the same whether you plan to stay for one, two, or all three nights. Children ages 17 and under stay for free.

This fee includes: Lodging for Thursday, Friday and Saturday nights, some meals & access to amenities, as well as use of kayaks, canoes, tennis courts, game room, volleyball courts, beach and yard games.

Meals Included: Breakfast daily, Lunch on Saturday, and dinner Friday and Saturday nights.
